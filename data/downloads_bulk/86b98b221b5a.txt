城市
1 贝洛奥里藏特 - 全国第三大城市，米納斯吉拉斯的首府。
2 尼泰罗伊  - The city across the Guanabara Bay from Rio de Janeiro. There are beautiful beaches and mountains you can go to the top of them and view all the beauty of Rio. A visit to Itacoatiara beach is recommended.
3 欧鲁普雷图  - Most beautiful colonial town in Brazil, has a large collection of Baroque art of the 16th and 17th centuries, the golden age in the country. Monumental churches, museums and urban tracks are now protected as World Heritage Site by UNESCO.
4 帕拉蒂  - Quaint coastal colonial town with cobblestone streets, hiking options, and access to hundreds of small islands in the Bay of Ilha Grande
5 里约热内卢 - Brazil's "marvelous city", with famous beaches such as Copacabana and Ipanema
6 圣保罗 - Largest city in South America, a sprawling metropolis with a vast array of cultural, culinary, and shopping options.
7 桑托斯  -  圣保罗州重要的港口城市，巴西球员贝利曾在该地的足球俱乐部踢球。
8 Tiradentes  -  米納斯吉拉斯的历史城镇。
9 维多利亚  - 聖埃斯皮里圖的首府。It is famous for its beautiful bay, the alongside Convento da Penha (a hilltop Franciscan convent dating from the 16th century), its beaches and nightlife.
